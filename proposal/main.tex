\begin{abstract}
This research proposal outlines a comprehensive approach to developing automatic mixing systems for music production. The proposed work aims to advance the state-of-the-art in audio processing by creating intelligent systems that can automatically balance and enhance multi-track recordings, reducing the time and expertise required for professional-quality music production.
\end{abstract}
