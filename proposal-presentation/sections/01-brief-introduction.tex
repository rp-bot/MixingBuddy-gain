\column{0.55\textwidth}
\begin{itemize}
    \item<1-> \textbf{Music mixing} requires a complex, relational understanding of multiple audio tracks.
    \item<2-> This research investigates a framework to fine-tune an \textbf{Audio-Language Model (ALM)} to generate actionable mixing advice.
    \item<2-> As a starting point, we condition the model on an \textbf{``anchor track''} (e.g., bass) to teach it how to balance the levels of other instruments relative to that \textbf{stable reference point}.
\end{itemize}
