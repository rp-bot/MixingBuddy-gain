\column{0.55\textwidth}
\begin{itemize}
    \item<1-> \textbf{Music mixing} requires \textbf{expertise} and a \textbf{complex, relational understanding} of multiple audio tracks.
    \item<2-> This research develops a \textbf{multimodal} system that leverages a \textbf{pre-trained LLM} to analyze \textbf{raw audio} and provide actionable feedback on \textbf{flawed mixes}.
    \item<3-> As a starting point, we focus on generating advice for \textbf{gain-balancing} only, with \textbf{audio-only} input.
\end{itemize}
