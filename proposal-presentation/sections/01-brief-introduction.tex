\begin{itemize}
    \item<1-> \textbf{Music mixing} requires \textbf{expertise} and a \textbf{complex, relational understanding} of multiple audio tracks.
    \item<2-> This research develops a \textbf{multimodal} system that equips a \textbf{pre-trained LLM} with the ability to analyze \textbf{raw audio}, allowing it to provide actionable feedback on \textbf{flawed mixes}.
    \item<2-> As a starting point, we focus on generating advice for \textbf{gain-balancing} only.
\end{itemize}
